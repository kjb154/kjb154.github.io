Whoelsale Divison

Our wholesale team works to aquire bellow market homes in upencoming areas across the country. To find these homes we use a unique filtering system which has set requirements of specific home atributes that we have found work best with our buisness model. After creating a list of homes that meet our requirements we reach out to the owners of the homes to see if they are intrested in selling. If they are we contact them within the next 24 hours with an offer we calculate from a unique formula we perfected to give the seller a fair price as well as maximizug the potential of the investment once we aquire the home.

Wholesale Steps:

  1. Find homes using unique filtering method
  2. Call home owners to find if they are willing to sell
  3. Aquire homes under contract and assign contract to a cash buyer

Fix and Flip Divsion

Our fix and flip team is the second of our two real estate teams. They also apply a filter to serch for homes, however the homes our fix and flip team looks for are delpaidated, have structural problems, and are sometimes unlivable. Their goals is to look at home that need to be worked on before being sold again. They target delapidated homes in good neihboorhoods. After aquireing homes they spend 6-12 months fixing a home before putting it back on the market.
